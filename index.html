Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Paradise AI Marketing Engine

How quickly can I set up the AI chatbot?

Setup takes less than 5 minutes! We've designed our platform to be incredibly user-friendly. Simply connect your business information, upload your service details and pricing, and the AI learns everything it needs to know. No coding required, no technical expertise needed. Most businesses are capturing leads within the same hour they sign up.

What if the AI makes a mistake or gives wrong information?

Our AI is trained specifically on your business information, so it only provides accurate details about your services, pricing, and policies. For complex questions outside its knowledge base, it smoothly hands off the conversation to your team. You maintain complete control and can review conversations anytime. Our system continuously learns and improves from each interaction, becoming smarter over time. Plus, you can set up guardrails and approval workflows for sensitive information.

Is my customer data secure and private?

Absolutely. We take data security and privacy extremely seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using enterprise-grade encryption standards. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and all major privacy regulations. Your customer data is never shared with third parties, never used for training other models, and is completely under your control. We undergo regular security audits and maintain SOC 2 compliance. You can also request data deletion at any time.

How much does it cost and what's included?

We offer flexible pricing based on your business size and lead volume. Our starter plan begins at $99/month and includes unlimited AI conversations, basic analytics, and email support. Professional plans ($299/month) add advanced features like multi-channel integration, priority support, and custom training. Enterprise plans are customized for larger organizations. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Most businesses see ROI within 30 days, so the investment pays for itself quickly through increased conversions and efficiency.

Will the AI work for my specific type of business?

Yes! Our AI has been successfully implemented across dozens of industries: real estate, home services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical), salons and spas, fitness centers, dental practices, legal services, e-commerce, restaurants, professional services, and more. Every business that sells services or products and wants to capture more leads benefits from our platform. During onboarding, we customize the AI specifically for your industry and business model. If you're unsure, our team is happy to discuss your specific use case and show you examples from similar businesses in your industry.
